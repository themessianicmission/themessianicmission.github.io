Love Thy Neighbor

The idea behind The Messianic Mission is to create a bridge between communities, bringing them together under the teachings of Jesus Christ.

The idea is simple: Love Thy Neighbor. The Golden Rule—the foundation of all law, the ultimate commandment. Bring peace to your heart by letting go of all hate, leaving only love for those around you and beyond. Pray away the aching memories of past faults and focus solely on the future, embracing each passing night as a renewal of spiritual purity through the Psalms. Now is the time.
