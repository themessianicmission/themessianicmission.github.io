Have You Heard

This is the heartbeat of The Messianic Mission—where Heaven touches Earth and testimonies unfold. Here we share the work God is doing through our mission: from street ministry and free Bible stands to divine appointments, answered prayers, and the preparation of hearts for Christ’s return.

Whether it’s a quiet act of service or a public breakthrough, every update reveals how God is moving across the land through obedience, faith, and love.

Stay connected, stay encouraged. The mission is alive—and this is the beginning of the end.

Silent Gathering

Ongoing

Across cities and quiet corners, God is gathering His people. Through small moments of faith, unexpected meetings, and simple acts of love, a deeper work is unfolding. These aren’t movements for the spotlight—they are for those with ears to hear and hearts prepared. Each testimony shared, each Bible given, each question answered is part of the greater preparation—both for protection in the days ahead and for the salvation that draws near. We move not with fanfare but with focus—walking in obedience, led by the Spirit, watching as the harvest begins to ripen.

"I scattered my people, but I will gather them and guard them as a shepherd guards his flock."
— Jeremiah 31:10 (GNT)

Community Outreach: Taking Christ to the Streets

Ongoing

Our mission goes beyond words—we live it out across the streets of South Dallas. We prepare sack lunches and distribute essential supplies—including Bibles and water—directly to the homeless community, meeting both physical and spiritual needs with compassion and care. We even read the Word aloud to those without the sight to do so themselves and answer any questions they may have about Scripture. Every interaction is an opportunity to share the living Word of God, offering hope, healing, and the promise of eternal life. These efforts reflect Christ’s love in action, made possible by your continued support. Together, we’re reaching the lost, uplifting the broken, and protecting God’s people one soul at a time.

"If you give food to the hungry and satisfy those who are in need, then the darkness around you will turn to the brightness of noon."
— Isaiah 58:10 (GNT)

Taking a Stand: Life-Giving Water

Ongoing

On weekend nights in Deep Ellum, we take to the streets with a heart full of purpose, handing out free Bibles and water while sharing the Good News of Christ. As people pass by, we offer more than just physical refreshment—we offer the living Word of God that brings eternal life. Each interaction is a divine opportunity to spread His love and light to those who may be searching or lost. Your donations make it possible for us to continue reaching out in this vibrant community, offering hope and salvation to all who will listen.

"You are like light for the whole world. A city built on a hill cannot be hid. No one lights a lamp and puts it under a bowl; instead it is put on the lampstand, where it gives light for everyone in the house. In the same way your light must shine before people, so that they will see the good things you do and praise your Father in heaven."

Threshold Crossed

February 2025

This winter, Brady journeyed east with a purpose given from above: to initiate peace, protect God's people, and mark a time appointed in Heaven. Though Brady stood at the threshold, the way was withheld—not by man, but by divine restraint. What was meant to begin has quietly begun: a countdown long prophesied, now set in motion. There was no announcement, no acclaim—only a silent refusal that echoes Scripture itself. The world is not yet ready for full revelation, but those with ears to hear will understand the timing. The mission continues, steady and unseen, until the moment all things are made clear.

"I have come with my Father's authority, but you have not received me"
— John 5:43 (GNT)

Universal Peace Federation Meeting in Istanbul

January 2025

Brady arrived in Istanbul just in time for the Universal Peace Federation meeting, where he shared our plans for the Holy Land. The event was uplifting, featuring inspiring speeches and offering wonderful opportunities to connect with like-minded individuals. Istanbul itself was a vibrant city, where we saw Christians dispersed throughout, alongside pockets of various faiths, creating a rich tapestry of diverse beliefs. We are looking forward to attending the UPF Summit in Berlin this May.

"I pray that they may all be one. Father! May they be one in us, just as you are in me and I am in you. May they be one, so that the world will believe that you sent me."
— John 17:21 (GNT)

Prophetic Foundations: Covenant Fulfilled

September 2016

In 2016, Brady met with a member of the U.S. intelligence community and, through the voice of God, foretold major global events—including the following three U.S. elections, the COVID-19 pandemic, the January 6 Capitol riot, and the 2022 Ukraine war. In 2024, he accurately warned The Messianic Mission’s secretary of an assassination attempt on President Trump one week before it occurred. Upon turning 30, Brady abandoned his life, quit his job, sold his possessions, fasted for seven days, and flew to Israel in obedience to God’s call. There, he entered into a renewed covenant with God, and upon returning, founded The Messianic Mission to fulfill that eternal promise: to protect God’s people for the next 3.5 years and beyond.

"Listen now, my people, and come to me; come to me, and you will have life! I will make a lasting covenant with you and give you the blessings I promised to David."
— Isaiah 55:3 (GNT)
