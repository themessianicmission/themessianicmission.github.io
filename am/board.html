Meet Our Board

Founder and President

Brady Schappert

"I have sacrificed every aspect of my life in full devotion to God."

Brady walks the land as a living witness, led by God to awaken, prepare, and establish the Kingdom on earth as it is in heaven. Having walked through death and returned with vision, he leads The Messianic Mission with unwavering obedience, spiritual clarity, and a calling to unify the scattered, stir the sleeping, and boldly proclaim the truth of Christ. As the founder, Brady serves as a servant—guided directly by the Word of God, beyond systems of man, time, or decay. He plants flags where the Spirit leads and walks in constant communion with the will of his Father.

Vice-President

Ronnie Sekishiro

"I was reborn to answer the call of our Lord."

Ronnie serves with a heart forged in loyalty, discernment, and unshakable faith. A trusted brother and steadfast witness, Ronnie walks alongside Brady, bearing the weight of this mission with strength and humility. He guards the vision, supports the movement with boldness, and stands as a pillar of integrity through every challenge. Ronnie’s voice carries truth, his steps are ordered by the Spirit, and his commitment to God’s people reflects a life surrendered to the call. In silence or speech, he leads with presence, prayer, and purpose.

Secretary

Molly McCray

"I believe in the sole promotion of peace and prosperity through Love Himself."

Molly serves with clarity, grace, and spiritual attentiveness. Entrusted with the careful stewardship of records and communication, she ensures that every word, every detail, and every movement aligns with the mission's divine calling. Her role goes beyond administration—she is a guardian of order, a vessel of peace, and a quiet force of stability. Molly’s dedication reflects a heart attuned to God’s timing, and her faithfulness in the unseen moments strengthens the foundation of everything seen.
